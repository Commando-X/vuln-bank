Privacy Policy

Last updated: whenever we felt like it. Effective: immediately and retroactively.

1. What We Collect

Everything. Your username, your password (stored in glorious plaintext, by the way), your account balance, your transaction history, and anything else you're kind enough to type into our input fields. We also collect whatever our SSRF vulnerability decides to fetch on your behalf.

2. How We Store It

In a SQLite database with all the security of a screen door on a submarine. Passwords are not hashed because we believe in transparency. Your JWT tokens live in localStorage because cookies felt too mainstream.

3. Who Can Access Your Data

Anyone, really. Our IDOR vulnerabilities ensure that if someone can guess your account number, they can check your balance. Our admin panel URL is just obscure enough to feel secure but obvious enough for anyone with a wordlist. We call this "security through mild inconvenience."

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do, however, make it freely available to anyone who knows how to craft a path traversal in our profile picture upload, enumerate our API endpoints, or simply inject a bit of creative SQL into the right input field.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to be vulnerable. You have the right to an account that can be taken over. You have the right to watch your balance change in ways you did not authorize. If you do not have a penetration testing tool, one will be appointed to you by the security community.

6. Contact

If you have concerns about your privacy, we recommend not using this application. Alternatively, file an issue on our GitHub repository and we will address it with the same urgency we apply to input validation.
