Terms of Service

By using VulnBank, you agree to the following terms. By "agree," we mean you probably didn't read this.

1. Acceptance of Risk

By creating an account, you acknowledge that your authentication token will be stored in localStorage, accessible to any XSS payload that wanders through. You accept this with the same enthusiasm you accept cookie banners.

2. Account Security

We provide no CSRF protection on any form. This means that if you visit a malicious website while logged in, someone could transfer your entire balance to themselves. We consider this a feature that encourages mindful browsing habits.

3. Cross-Site Scripting

Several fields in our application render user input via innerHTML without sanitization. If you encounter JavaScript executing where you didn't expect it, congratulations, you've found a feature. Your username field is particularly adventurous in this regard.

4. Password Policy

There is no password policy. Your password can be "a" and we will accept it with open arms and store it in plaintext. We believe password complexity requirements are a form of gatekeeping.

5. Rate Limiting

Our login endpoint has no rate limiting. Brute force away. We view this as our commitment to accessibility for password-guessing enthusiasts.

6. Liability

VulnBank is liable for absolutely nothing. This application was built to be broken. If you deposit real money into a demo application called "Vulnerable Bank," the resulting financial loss is technically a lesson in reading comprehension.

7. Modifications

We reserve the right to introduce new vulnerabilities at any time without notice. We also reserve the right to accidentally fix vulnerabilities, though historically this has not been a concern.
